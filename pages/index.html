Our Vision

The Wireless, Audio, Vision, and Electronics for Sensing (WAVES) Lab delves into the fascinating world of imperceptible signals. We harness the power of radio wave, sound, light, and electronic components to sense and understand our environment in new and innovative ways.

Our lab fosters a collaborative spirit, bringing together experts in various disciplines to push the boundaries of technology. Whether it's using sound waves to map a room or harnessing the power of vision for object recognition, we explore the potential of these technologies to create a richer and more connected world.
