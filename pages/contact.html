Join Us

We are excited to welcome passionate and dedicated students to join our lab! Here are the steps to get started, depending on the program you are interested in:

PhD Applicants:

If you are interested in pursuing a PhD in our lab, please apply directly to the CIS or ESE department through the online application portal. In the portal, as well as your Statement of Purpose (SOP), be sure to mention Prof. Mingmin Zhao and express your specific research interests and how they align with our lab's work. This will ensure that your application is reviewed with your interests in mind.

Master and Undergraduate Students:

For those interested in Master or Undergraduate research opportunities in our lab, please send an email to Prof. Mingmin Zhao. In your email, include your CV and a brief description of your research interests and any relevant experience. This will help us understand your background and how you might contribute to our ongoing projects.

We look forward to learning more about you and potentially welcoming you to our research team!
